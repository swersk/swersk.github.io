Why the Paleo Diet is BS

<p>http://www.motherjones.com/environment/2014/01/michael-pollan-paleo-diet-inquiring-minds</p><p></p><p>The more I read about food, the more I am convinced that diets don't work. I am suspicious of any diet, trend, or anything that tells you to restrict a certain food group. The Southbeach diet, the no-carb diet, and now the Paleo Diet are all unsustainable, restrictive ways to go about eating. I encourage you to read this article; Michael Pollan has a way of explaining complex ideas simply. If you don't feel like reading it though, here's a quick summary:</p><p></p><p>-Justifying daily consumption of meat  by saying our hunter-gatherer ancestors did the same is wrong; our ancestors rarely ate meat, and they didn't have certain enzymes that we have today.</p><p></p><p>-We shouldn't completely avoid bread. Bread has nutrients from grains. Whole wheat and whole grain bread are rich in fiber.</p><p></p><p>-Fermented foods are good for us.</p><p></p><p>-A 100% raw food diet is not 100% healthy. Cooking can actually bring out nutrients in food.</p><p></p><p>-Cook! A cooked meal is almost always healthier than eating out.</p>
