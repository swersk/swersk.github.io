Coursera

Coursera envisions a world in which anyone, anywhere can transform their life by accessing the world's best learning experience.

At Coursera, I:

  • managed the beta testing process to ensure high-quality product launch
  • advised ~100 universities on their education offerings, curriculum design and learning strategy
  • created content for Coursera for Refugees, a portal designed for ~30,000 refugee learners. I presented the work at the EMOOCS 2017 conference in Madrid.
  • launched Coursera for Brazil
  • reviewed learner analytics and conducted usability testing
  • trained professors how to create pedagogically-sound online courses
