Advising Harvard's Education Strategy

I researched the use of partnerships and networks in Harvard University's global education strategy. Based on qualitative research findings, I developed recommendations for improving the use of partnerships and their leadership implications.I conducted qualitative research to understand the role of partnerships and networks in Harvard University's global education strategy. I used the results to develop recommendations for improving the use of partnerships and their leadership implications.More detail: <a href="https://laurenswersky.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/harvard-international-student-experiences1.pdf">International Partnerships for Student Learning at Harvard</a>.
