UNICEF

They say it doesn't feel like work when you're doing something you believe in. That's why I enjoyed working in partnership with UNICEF to advise on China's innovative K-12 education program on social and emotional learning (SEL). More about the project:
  • Social and Emotional Learning Programs: A literature review on social and emotional learning curricula and programs.
  • P.A.T.H.S to Adulthood- A Case Study: A case study of Hong Kong's P.A.T.H.S SEL program that attempts to develop "21st century skills" using Pellegrino and Hilton's framework. Included is a proposal on how to effectively evaluate the program.
  • Transferring Project P.A.T.H.S. to Mainland China- Small Adjustments, Large Gains: An exploration of the relevancy and feasibility of transferring SEL programs to mainland China as aligned with UNICEF's education strategy.
  • UNICEF vs. World Bank: A synthesis of the comparative education strategies of UNICEF and the World Bank.
