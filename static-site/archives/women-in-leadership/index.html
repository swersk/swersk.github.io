Women in Leadership Speech

During my teaching fellowship with the Harvard Center for Asia Leadership Initiatives, I gave TED-style speeches to groups of 50–300 students and young professionals about women in leadership. In my speech I encouraged women to stand up for and believe in themselves, and I exhorted men to support the women in their lives. Echoing Joe Biden's platform, I believe every issue is a women’s issue — health care, the economy, education, national security. I believe we need an aggressive and comprehensive plan to further women’s economic and physical security and ensure full inclusion of and equality for women. Below are the slides from the speech.
